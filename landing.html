Handloom Clothes

Handloom is a traditional weaving craft practiced by generations of artsians to create attractrive textile designs.Handloom fabrics are generally made with high quality natural fibres like cotton,lien,silk and wool which are resilient and last for a long time.The handloom sector plays a very important role in the country's economy.The importance of handloom sector in the national economy cannot be overemphasised on account of having the advantage of flexibility of small production run,uniqueness,innovation and adaptability to the exports requirement.
